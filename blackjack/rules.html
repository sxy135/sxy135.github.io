The Rules of the Game

Unfamiliar how to play? No worries! The rules are right here, easy and simple to follow!

The Value of the Cards

  • Number cards are face value.
  • Jacks, Queens, and Kings have a value of 10.
  • Aces can have a value of 11 or 1.

How to Play

The basics

  • The Player (That's you!) places a bet.
  • Cards are dealt: two for the player, two for the dealer (only one of the dealer's will be initially visible until you stand).
  • You may stand and receive no more cards, or hit for another card.
  • If the dealer has a total value of 16 or less, the dealer must keep hitting until a value of 17 or higher is reached. Then the dealer stands.
  • The closest to 21 "wins".
  • If either you or the dealer goes over 21, it's an automatic "loss", called a bust.
  • If the player and dealer end up with the same value, it is a "tie".
